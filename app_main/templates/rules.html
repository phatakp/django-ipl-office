Registration and Stakes
  1. Interested players to register on the site here. (via personal devices only).
  2. Upon registration each player will be allocated 2000 points as starting balance.
  3. Player needs to predict the winner of every match before the cutoff.
  4. Cutoff time for prediction for any match is 1 hour prior the start time of match.
    • For matches starting at 3:30 PM IST, prediction should be done before 2:30 PM IST.
    • For matches starting at 7:30 PM IST, prediction should be done before 6:30 PM IST.
  5. While predicting, you will automatically stake certain points from your kitty as per below scenario.
    • For first 28 league matches - 20 points
    • For next 28 league matches - 30 points
    • For Qualifiers and Eliminator - 40 points
    • For IPL Final - 150 points
  6. You will be able to modify your prediction up until start of the match (3:30 or 7:30) respectively. However, this will result in deduction of 10 points for every change .
  7. If you miss the cutoff for prediction, you will automatically forfeit applicable points for that match.
  8. The cutoff times for predictions will not change even if the match is delayed due to any circumstances.
  9. During registration, player needs to predict Winner of IPL-2021.
  10. At this point you will automatically stake additional 300 points in an IPL kitty, to be won at the end.
    This means your actual point balance after registration will be 1700.
  11. You will get an option to change the winner for IPL-2021, up until the completion of Match No.28 - RR vs SRH on Dashboard tab (IPL Winner Button) .
  12. If you change the winner before completion of Match No.12 - CSK vs RR, no points will be deducted from your account.
  13. However, starting Match No.13, points equivalent to No of Completed Matches x 5 will be deducted.
    • For e.g If you change IPL winner after completion of Match No.20, 100 points (20x5) will be deducted.
    • Similarly if you change winner after completion of Match No.25, 125 points (25x5) will be deducted.
  14. These points will be pooled in and will be added to whoever has guessed the correct IPL-2021 winners' kitty.
  15. In case of multiple winners (same team guessed by multiple),total points will be distributed evenly among them.
Gaining Points
  1. Settlement of points for every match will done as below -
  2. If your prediction was incorrect or you missed the cutoff for prediction, you lose the points that were applicable for the match.
  3. If your prediction was correct, you will receive points equivalent to (Total Staked points for match / Total number of winners).
    • For e.g. If suppose there are 20 registered players and 20 points applicable as individual stake for a match, then Total Stake = 20 x 20 = 400 points.
      If 5 players predicted the winner correctly, each of them get 400/5 = 80 points.
  4. In case the match is abandoned or no winning team could be identified due to any circumstances, the total points at stake gets added to the IPL winner kitty.
  5. For IPL Final, if there is no player, who correctly guessed the winner (during registration), the initial stake for 300 points in kitty will be null and void.
  6. However, in above scenario if there is any balance in IPL kitty due to change of IPL winner team or abandoned match,
    the total points in pool will be equally distributed to any players who did not change their overall IPL winner prediction throughout the course.
Prediction Process
  1. You can predict for a match from Dashboard tab (Next Match Button) or Fixtures & Results tab.
  2. All predictions from other players, will be visible only after the cutoff for match has passed.
  3. You will be displayed certain stats and winning percentage of teams for a match (based on a naive prediction model), to help you make a choice.
    However keep in mind, that lesser the number of correct predictions for any match, greater is the number of points the winner receives. So choose wisely
